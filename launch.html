30-second pitch:
HopWire is a platform that helps breweries promote events like trivia nights, live music, and beer releases in one place. Instead of posts getting lost on social media, everything lives in a single feed where customers can actually discover what’s happening near them.

How it works

At launch (late July–early August), participating breweries will receive a short enrollment window to create their account, select their plan, and lock in Founding pricing.

To join, email jbucher@hopwire.app with your brewery name and number of locations.

Only breweries that complete signup during the launch window will qualify for Founding Brewery Partner pricing.

Our commitment

HopWire is built to help breweries grow by making event promotion easier and more effective. As the platform expands, Founding Breweries will always retain their pricing and full access as a thank-you for supporting us early.
