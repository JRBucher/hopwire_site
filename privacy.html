HopWire Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Welcome to HopWire ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use the HopWire mobile application and website (collectively, the "Service"). Please read this policy carefully. If you disagree with its terms, please discontinue use of the Service.

1. Information We Collect

Information You Provide Directly

  • Account Information: Name, email, city, age, and password when you register.
  • Profile Information: Profile photo, bio, and any other details you choose to add.
  • User Content: Messages, photos, and other content you submit to the community.
  • Payment Information: If you are a brewery owner, billing details are collected and processed by our third-party payment processor. We do not store your full payment card details.
  • Communications: Messages you send us directly, such as support requests, feedback, or optional responses provided when requesting account deletion.

Information Collected Automatically

  • Usage Data: Pages viewed, features used, time spent in the app, and interactions with content.
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, unique device identifiers, and mobile network information.
  • Log Data: IP address, browser type, referring URLs, and crash reports.
  • Cookies & Tracking Technologies: We use cookies and similar technologies to operate and improve the Service.

Information from Third Parties

If you register or log in using a third-party service (such as Apple or Google), we may receive basic profile information from that provider in accordance with their privacy policies.

Facebook Page Integration

If you are a brewery or business and choose to connect your Facebook Page to HopWire, we access certain information through Facebook's Graph API, with your permission, in order to import your events into HopWire. Specifically, we collect:

  • Page identity: The list of Facebook Pages you manage and the name and ID of the Page you connect.
  • Page access token: A secure token that allows HopWire to read the connected Page's content on your behalf. This token is stored securely and used only to retrieve your events.
  • Event data: Event titles, descriptions, dates and times, and cover images posted on your connected Page.

We use this information solely to display your Facebook events within HopWire alongside events you create directly. We do not post to your Facebook Page, modify your Page, or access personal profile content beyond what is described here. You can disconnect your Facebook Page at any time, and you may request deletion of imported data and your stored access token by contacting us at info@hopwire.app.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Create and manage your account
  • Provide, operate, and improve the Service
  • Personalize your experience and surface relevant community content
  • Import and display events from your connected Facebook Page
  • Process transactions and manage your subscription
  • Send you service-related notices, updates, and security alerts
  • Respond to your comments, questions, and support requests
  • Monitor and enforce our Community Guidelines and Terms of Service
  • Detect, investigate, and prevent fraudulent or unauthorized activity
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information in the following limited circumstances:

  • Service Providers: We work with trusted third-party vendors (e.g., hosting, analytics, payment processing, email delivery) who access your data only to perform services on our behalf.
  • Other Users: Content you post publicly on HopWire (messages, photos, profile information) is visible to other users of the Service.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose your information if required by law, subpoena, or other legal process, or if we believe disclosure is necessary to protect our rights or the safety of others.
  • Business Transfers: If HopWire is involved in a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction. We will notify you before your data becomes subject to a different privacy policy.

4. Data Retention and Deletion

We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the Service.

When you request deletion of your account, your account is deactivated and your profile and content are no longer visible to other users. We then permanently delete or anonymize your personal data within 30 days of your request, except where we are required to retain certain information for legal, security, or legitimate business purposes (such as records of transactions).

For brewery accounts with an active paid subscription, an account deletion request also serves as notice of subscription cancellation. The subscription will not renew, and account data is removed following the end of the final billing period.

Facebook Page access tokens are revoked and deleted, along with imported event data, when you disconnect your Page or request deletion of your account.

5. Your Choices and Rights

  • Access & Correction: You can review and update your account information at any time through your profile settings.
  • Account Deletion: You may request deletion of your account at any time through your account settings, or by emailing us at info@hopwire.app. Your account will be deactivated and your data removed as described in Section 4. If you are a brewery owner with an active paid subscription, your deletion request also cancels your subscription effective at the end of the current billing period.
  • Disconnect Facebook: You may disconnect your Facebook Page at any time, which stops further event imports and allows you to request deletion of your stored access token.
  • Opt-Out of Marketing Emails: You can unsubscribe from promotional emails using the link at the bottom of any such email.
  • Do Not Track: Some browsers include a "Do Not Track" feature. We currently do not respond to Do Not Track signals, but we do not sell your data to third parties.

6. Children's Privacy

HopWire is not intended for people under the age of 21. We do not knowingly collect personal information from people under 21. If we become aware that a person under 21 has provided us with personal information, we will delete it promptly and delete their account. If you believe a person under 21 has submitted information to us, please contact us at info@hopwire.app.

7. Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Third-Party Links

The Service may contain links to third-party websites or services. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those third parties and encourage you to review their privacy policies.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. For significant changes, we will notify you via email or a prominent notice within the app. Your continued use of the Service after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
